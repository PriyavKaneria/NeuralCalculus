title: Make a business out of your life
categories: [life]
tags: [business, poetry]
---

how to make a successful business?
pick a problem you would love to solve
plan out what is the standard way to solve it
learn theory, do hands on
experiment with new approaches
have friends that support, make friends that are useful
figure out some niche
master the ways for that
make something unique that people chose you
make money, enough to survive and grow
expand your fields, explore similar domains
capture them and innovate
expand expand expand
become the jack of all trades and master of one
let someone else be the master of your trade
become a monopoly at a full set of skills
find what you love, do what you love, spend time with friends
teach the next generations
live and die

how to live a happy and grateful life?
